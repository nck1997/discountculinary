Garlic-Herb Flatbreads

Soft skillet or oven flatbreads with garlic and herb flavor, useful for wraps, dips, or quick pizza bases.

Servings6
Calories / serving200
Protein / serving6g
Total1200 kcal · 34g

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Greek yogurt or water depending on version
  • Salt
  • Baking powder optional
  • Garlic powder
  • Parsley or mixed herbs
  • Olive oil or butter

Steps

  1. Mix dough ingredients and knead briefly.
  2. Rest 10 to 20 minutes.
  3. Divide and roll thin.
  4. Cook in hot skillet or bake until puffed and spotted.
  5. Brush with garlic-herb butter or oil.

Notes

Can sit between naan, pita, and yogurt flatbread depending on hydration.
