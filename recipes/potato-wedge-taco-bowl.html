Potato Wedge Taco Bowl

Seasoned ground beef and crispy air fryer potato wedges served bowl-style with fresh avocado, skyr chipotle crema, and Mexican cheeses.

Servings2
Calories / serving868
Protein / serving58g
Total1735 kcal · 116g

Ingredients

  • --- BEEF ---
  • 1 lb 93/7 ground beef (or 85/15 if that's what you have)
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp taco seasoning (homemade or store-bought)
  • Splash of beef bone broth
  • 1 tsp avocado oil
  • --- POTATO WEDGES ---
  • 1.5 lbs baby potatoes or fingerlings, halved
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt to taste
  • --- BOWL ASSEMBLY ---
  • 1 avocado, halved and sliced
  • Skyr chipotle crema (see recipe)
  • Cotija cheese
  • Mexican cheese blend
  • Fresh cilantro, torn
  • Fire sauce or hot sauce to finish

Steps

  1. Toss halved potatoes with olive oil, smoked paprika, garlic powder, chili powder, oregano, and salt.
  2. Air fry at 400°F for 18-22 minutes, shaking halfway, until crispy and golden. Can be made ahead and reheated.
  3. Heat avocado oil in a skillet over medium-high. Cook onion and garlic until golden, about 4 minutes.
  4. Add ground beef, break up, and cook through.
  5. Add taco seasoning and a splash of beef bone broth. Stir and simmer until liquid cooks down.
  6. If using pre-made potato wedges, return them to the same pan after removing the beef. Deglaze with a splash of bone broth to pick up the beef fond and toss the potatoes until coated.
  7. Add beef and potatoes to a large bowl. Fan sliced avocado over one side. Spoon skyr chipotle crema alongside.
  8. Top with cotija, Mexican cheese blend, and torn cilantro. Finish potatoes with fire sauce.

Notes

The reheat trick — deglazing the beef pan with bone broth and tossing the potatoes in the fond — is what makes the potatoes exceptional. Don't skip it if you're using pre-made wedges. Baby potato medley or fingerlings hold their shape best; gold potatoes work but can be softer.
