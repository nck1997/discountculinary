Spaghetti alla Carbonara

Classic-ish carbonara approach with eggs, cheese, black pepper, and rendered meat fat for sauce body.

Servings4
Calories / serving538
Protein / serving24g
Total2150 kcal · 95g

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan or pecorino
  • Black pepper
  • Guanciale, pancetta, bacon, or a substitute meat
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cook pasta in salted water.
  2. Render meat until crisp and reserve some fat.
  3. Whisk eggs, cheese, and black pepper.
  4. Toss hot pasta with meat and a little fat off heat.
  5. Temper with pasta water and add egg mixture while stirring constantly.
  6. Adjust with more pasta water until glossy.

Notes

Needs heat control. Too hot and it scrambles, too cool and it clumps.
