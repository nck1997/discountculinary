Butter Chicken (WIP)

Yogurt-marinated chicken in a spiced tomato-cream sauce. Good but not yet dialed in — currently runs too acidic. Next attempt: cashew paste and a pinch of sugar to balance. The marinade is solid; the sauce needs refinement.

Servings3
Calories / serving575
Protein / serving62g
Total1725 kcal · 186g

Ingredients

  • --- MARINADE ---
  • 1.5 lbs chicken breast, cubed and scored
  • 1/2 cup whole milk yogurt (or 0% skyr + 1 tbsp heavy cream)
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp avocado oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced (or ginger garlic paste)
  • 1 tbsp fresh ginger, grated
  • 1.5 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1.5 tsp Kashmiri chili powder (or paprika + pinch cayenne)
  • 1 tsp coriander
  • Salt to taste
  • --- SAUCE ---
  • 2 tbsp butter (split)
  • 1 tbsp neutral oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced fine
  • 5-6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes (14oz) — San Marzano preferred
  • 1.5 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp coriander
  • 1 tsp Kashmiri chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Pinch of sugar (next time — helps acidity)
  • Cashew paste — 1/4 cup soaked raw cashews blended smooth (next time — key for creaminess)
  • 1/2 cup cottage cheese, blended smooth
  • 1 scoop Ancient Nutrition bone broth powder
  • 1 tbsp cold butter to finish
  • Kasuri methi (dried fenugreek) to finish
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken overnight — combine all marinade ingredients, coat chicken thoroughly, refrigerate.
  2. Cook marinated chicken in a screaming hot cast iron or under broiler until charred in spots. Set aside. Do not skip the char.
  3. In a wide pan, cook onion in 1 tbsp butter + oil over medium until deeply golden, 12-15 min minimum.
  4. Add garlic, ginger, cook 2 min.
  5. Add tomato paste, cook until darkened, 2 min.
  6. Add dry spices, toast 60 seconds.
  7. Add crushed tomatoes and pinch of sugar. Simmer 20-25 min until thickened and oil begins to separate.
  8. Blend sauce smooth with immersion blender.
  9. Stir in blended cottage cheese and cashew paste.
  10. Add charred chicken, simmer together 10-15 min.
  11. Stir in heavy cream and bone broth powder, simmer gently 5 min.
  12. Finish with cold butter off heat and kasuri methi.
  13. Adjust salt and serve.

Notes

WORK IN PROGRESS. Current issue: sauce runs too acidic/tomatoey. Fixes to try next time: (1) cashew paste — soak 1/4 cup raw cashews, blend smooth, add before cream; (2) pinch of sugar during tomato simmer; (3) San Marzano tomatoes instead of regular crushed; (4) longer tomato simmer before blending. The marinade is solid and does not need changes. The char step is non-negotiable. Milk and cream do not fix acidity — they only thin the sauce.
