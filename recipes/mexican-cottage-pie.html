Mexican Cottage Pie

A Mexican-inspired twist on the British classic — birria-spiced ground beef under a jalapeño cheddar mash. Baked in cast iron until the peaks go crispy.

Servings4
Calories / serving700
Protein / serving40g
Total2800 kcal · 160g

Ingredients

  • --- FILLING ---
  • 1 lb 93/7 ground beef
  • 1 Carroll Shelby's Birria Taco Seasoning packet
  • 1 yellow onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 3/4 cup beef stock
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • Salt and pepper
  • --- MASH ---
  • 2 lb russet or Yukon gold potatoes
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup Mexican 3-cheese blend
  • 1/2 cup sharp cheddar
  • 3 tbsp pickled jalapeños, roughly chopped
  • 1-2 tbsp chipotle mayo (stir into mash — highly recommended)
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Peel and cube potatoes. Add to cold salted water, bring to boil, cook until fork tender.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large oven-safe skillet over medium-high. Cook onion and garlic until softened, about 3 min.
  3. Add ground beef, break up, and brown fully. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Add tomato paste and cook into the meat for 1 minute.
  5. Add birria seasoning packet, Worcestershire, and beef stock. Simmer until liquid reduces into a thick gravy. Taste and adjust salt.
  6. Drain potatoes and mash well. Add cream, olive oil, chipotle mayo, and mash until smooth.
  7. Fold in both cheeses and pickled jalapeños. Taste — it should be well seasoned and a little spicy.
  8. Spoon mash over the beef filling and spread to the edges. Fork up the surface to create peaks.
  9. Bake at 400°F until the top is golden and crispy in spots.
  10. Rest 5 minutes before serving.

Notes

The birria packet does most of the seasoning heavy lifting — taste the filling before adding extra salt. The chipotle mayo in the mash is not in the original recipe but is the move — adds smokiness and richness that ties the whole thing together. Serve with Lime Sesame Toom Vinaigrette over baby spinach to cut through the richness. Leftovers reheat very well.
