Calzones

Folded pizza pockets using your protein pizza dough, filled with marinara, mozzarella, and meat or veg.

Servings4
Calories / serving550
Protein / serving42g
Total2200 kcal · 170g

Ingredients

  • High-protein pizza dough
  • Spicy marinara
  • Mozzarella
  • Parmesan or ricotta optional
  • Turkey ragu or other filling
  • Egg wash optional

Steps

  1. Divide and roll dough into circles.
  2. Add filling to one half, keeping edges clear.
  3. Fold over and crimp shut.
  4. Brush with egg wash if desired.
  5. Bake at 425°F until browned and cooked through.
  6. Rest a few minutes before cutting.

Notes

Great use for leftover turkey ragu or cheese-heavy fillings without making a full pizza.
