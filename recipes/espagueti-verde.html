Espagueti Verde

Creamy poblano green spaghetti with onion, garlic, crema-style body, and roasted pepper flavor.

Servings5
Calories / serving340
Protein / serving10g
Total1700 kcal · 52g

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Poblanos
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Cilantro optional
  • Mexican crema or Greek yogurt / cottage cheese swap
  • Milk or broth
  • Butter or oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Roast poblanos until blistered; steam and peel.
  2. Cook onion and garlic until softened.
  3. Blend poblanos with onion, garlic, crema or yogurt blend, and a splash of milk/broth.
  4. Cook spaghetti until al dente.
  5. Simmer sauce briefly, then toss with pasta.
  6. Season and serve.

Notes

Good as-is or with grilled chicken added for more protein.
