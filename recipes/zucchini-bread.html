Zucchini Bread

Moist quick bread that works as a lower-guilt breakfast bake or snack.

Servings10
Calories / serving210
Protein / serving3g
Total2100 kcal · 34g

Ingredients

  • Zucchini, grated
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Brown sugar
  • Oil or melted butter
  • Cinnamon
  • Vanilla
  • Baking soda
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F and grease loaf pan.
  2. Mix wet ingredients together.
  3. Fold in zucchini.
  4. Whisk dry ingredients separately, then combine gently.
  5. Bake until set and a tester comes out mostly clean.
  6. Cool before slicing.

Notes

Can be adjusted with walnuts or chocolate chips, but the simple version is already solid.
