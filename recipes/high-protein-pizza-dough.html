High-Protein Pizza Dough

Greek yogurt and bone broth powder boosted dough for thinner homemade pizzas or calzones.

Servings4
Calories / serving245
Protein / serving16g
Total980 kcal · 66g

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 scoop bone broth protein powder
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Extra flour for shaping

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients together.
  2. Fold in Greek yogurt and combine into a dough.
  3. Knead lightly until mostly smooth.
  4. Rest 10 to 15 minutes.
  5. Roll thin for pizza or divide for calzones.
  6. Par-bake if desired, then top and finish baking.

Notes

Great base for calzones or high-protein pizzas with turkey, chicken, or light cheese blends.
