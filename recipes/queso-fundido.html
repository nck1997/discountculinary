Queso Fundido

Melty cheese skillet with poblano and optional turkey chorizo or sausage for extra protein.

Servings6
Calories / serving275
Protein / serving18g
Total1650 kcal · 110g

Ingredients

  • Oaxaca cheese or mozzarella blend
  • Monterey Jack optional
  • Poblano
  • Onion
  • Turkey sausage or turkey chorizo optional
  • Oil
  • Tortillas or chips for serving

Steps

  1. Roast or char poblano, then peel and slice.
  2. Cook onion and sausage if using.
  3. Add peppers back in and top with cheese.
  4. Bake or cover in skillet until melted and bubbling.
  5. Serve hot with tortillas, chips, or flatbread.

Notes

Peak party food. Also works as a protein-heavy late meal if you keep the sausage in.
