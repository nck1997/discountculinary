Breakfast Tacos

Turkey sausage, eggs, cheese, and salsa breakfast tacos that actually hold up for repeat mornings.

Servings6
Calories / serving322
Protein / serving23g
Total1935 kcal · 137g

Ingredients

  • Turkey breakfast sausage
  • Eggs
  • Tortillas
  • Cheese
  • Red salsa
  • Optional: potatoes, onions, avocado

Steps

  1. Cook sausage and set aside.
  2. Scramble eggs softly.
  3. Warm tortillas.
  4. Fill with eggs, sausage, cheese, and salsa.
  5. Serve immediately.

Notes

Simple, reliable, and easy to macro-manage depending on tortilla and cheese choices.
