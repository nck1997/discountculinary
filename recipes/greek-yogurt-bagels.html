Greek Yogurt Bagels

Easy 2-ingredient style bagels with Greek yogurt and flour, great as a high-protein breakfast base.

Servings4
Calories / serving230
Protein / serving18g
Total920 kcal · 70g

Ingredients

  • 2 cups self-rising flour (or flour + baking powder + salt)
  • 1 1/2 cups plain nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 1 egg wash
  • Everything bagel seasoning or sesame seeds
  • Optional: pinch of salt if using homemade self-rising mix

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. Mix flour and Greek yogurt until a shaggy dough forms.
  3. Knead lightly until smooth enough to handle.
  4. Divide into 4 to 6 portions and shape into bagels.
  5. Brush with egg wash and top with seasoning.
  6. Bake until golden and cooked through, about 22 to 28 minutes.
  7. Cool slightly before slicing.

Notes

Good with egg sandwiches, smoked salmon, or breakfast sausage. Texture lands between a chewy roll and a classic bagel.
