Skyr Chipotle Crema

A high-protein sour cream substitute built on 0% skyr. Tangy, creamy, slightly smoky, and takes 2 minutes. Use anywhere you'd reach for sour cream or crema — tacos, taco bowls, cottage pie, eggs.

Servings3
Calories / serving112
Protein / serving8g
Total337 kcal · 23g

Ingredients

  • 1 cup 0% skyr (or plain Greek yogurt)
  • 1 tbsp Duke's mayo
  • 1 tbsp chipotle mayo
  • 1 tsp heavy cream
  • Pinch of salt to taste

Steps

  1. Combine skyr, Duke's mayo, chipotle mayo, and heavy cream in a small bowl.
  2. Stir until fully smooth and combined.
  3. Taste and adjust salt.
  4. Use immediately or refrigerate up to 4 days.

Notes

The Duke's mayo is what gives it body and that slight richness that plain Greek yogurt lacks. Chipotle mayo adds smokiness without heat. Works as a 1:1 swap for sour cream in basically any application.
