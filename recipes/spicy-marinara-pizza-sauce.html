Spicy Marinara / Pizza Sauce

Fast red sauce for pizza, calzones, pasta, or dipping, with garlic, tomato paste, and chili heat.

Servings6
Calories / serving53
Protein / serving1g
Total320 kcal · 8g

Ingredients

  • Crushed tomatoes or passata
  • Tomato paste
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Oregano
  • Basil
  • Red chili flakes
  • Salt
  • Pinch of sugar optional

Steps

  1. Cook garlic gently in olive oil.
  2. Stir in tomato paste and cook 1 to 2 minutes.
  3. Add tomatoes, oregano, basil, chili flakes, and salt.
  4. Simmer until slightly thickened.
  5. Blend if smoother texture is desired.

Notes

Keeps the flavor profile sharp and clean for pizzas and calzones without getting watery.
