Homemade Alfredo

Flexible Alfredo with cottage cheese or evaporated milk versions, built to stay creamy without being absurdly heavy.

Servings4
Calories / serving295
Protein / serving20g
Total1180 kcal · 82g

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Parmesan
  • Black pepper
  • Cottage cheese and milk OR evaporated milk
  • Pasta water
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Blend cottage cheese with milk if using that version.
  2. Warm butter and garlic gently.
  3. Add dairy base and heat carefully without breaking.
  4. Whisk in parmesan and black pepper.
  5. Use pasta water to loosen until glossy.
  6. Toss with hot pasta immediately.

Notes

You can lean richer or higher-protein depending on the dairy base. Great jumping-off point for chipotle or calabrian variants.
