High-Protein Calabrian Chicken Pasta

Creamy spicy chicken pasta with Calabrian chili heat and a lighter protein-forward sauce base.

Servings5
Calories / serving410
Protein / serving38g
Total2050 kcal · 190g

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast or thigh
  • Pasta
  • Calabrian chili paste
  • Garlic
  • Parmesan
  • Cottage cheese or Greek yogurt sauce base
  • Milk or broth
  • Butter or olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Season and cook chicken, then slice or chop.
  2. Cook pasta to al dente.
  3. Build sauce with garlic, chili paste, and blended dairy base.
  4. Add parmesan and thin with pasta water.
  5. Toss in pasta and chicken until coated.
  6. Adjust heat, salt, and texture.

Notes

A good weeknight bridge between vodka sauce energy and Alfredo structure, but higher protein.
