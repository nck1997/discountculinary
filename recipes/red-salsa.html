Red Salsa

Chile-forward salsa with dried chiles, garlic, onion, and a deeper roasted flavor.

Servings8
Calories / serving22
Protein / serving1g
Total180 kcal · 5g

Ingredients

  • Chile de árbol
  • Guajillo chiles
  • Roma tomatoes optional
  • Garlic
  • White onion
  • Salt
  • Water as needed
  • Optional: vinegar or bouillon pinch

Steps

  1. Toast chiles lightly until fragrant, being careful not to burn them.
  2. Soften chiles in hot water.
  3. Char onion, garlic, and tomatoes if using.
  4. Blend everything until smooth.
  5. Season with salt and thin to desired texture.
  6. Optional: simmer briefly to deepen flavor.

Notes

Works for tacos, eggs, queso fundido, grilled meats, or breakfast bowls.
