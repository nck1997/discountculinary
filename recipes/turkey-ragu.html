Turkey Ragu

Long-simmered spicy turkey ragu with onions, garlic, tomato paste, crushed tomatoes, herbs, and parmesan rind.

Servings6
Calories / serving317
Protein / serving35g
Total1900 kcal · 210g

Ingredients

  • 2 lb 93/7 ground turkey
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 5 to 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 to 3 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 large can crushed tomatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Oregano, basil, parsley
  • Red chili flakes
  • Smoked paprika
  • Parmesan rind optional
  • Salt and black pepper

Steps

  1. Brown turkey and season lightly, then remove.
  2. Cook onion in olive oil until softened and lightly golden.
  3. Add garlic and tomato paste; cook until darkened slightly.
  4. Add crushed tomatoes, herbs, chili flakes, paprika, and parmesan rind.
  5. Return turkey to the pot and simmer low for at least 45 to 90 minutes.
  6. Adjust seasoning and reduce until rich and concentrated.

Notes

Base recipe that can feed pasta, lasagna soup, baked pasta, or calzones. One of the core Water St utility sauces.
