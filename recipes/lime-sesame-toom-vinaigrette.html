Lime Sesame Toom Vinaigrette

A garlicky, acidic dressing that cuts through rich, heavy dishes. Built around toom and lime with a touch of sesame. Takes 2 minutes and punches way above its weight.

Servings1
Calories / serving45
Protein / serving0g
Total45 kcal · 0g

Ingredients

  • 1.5 tbsp fresh lime juice (about half a lime)
  • 1 tsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tsp toasted sesame oil
  • 1 tsp toom (Lebanese garlic sauce)
  • 1/2 tsp honey or sugar
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Combine lime juice, apple cider vinegar, sesame oil, and toom in a small bowl.
  2. Whisk until the toom emulsifies into the dressing.
  3. Add honey or sugar to balance the acid.
  4. Taste before adding salt — toom is already salty.
  5. Dress baby spinach or greens lightly right before serving so they don't wilt.
  6. Toss and serve immediately.

Notes

Go easy on the sesame oil — a little goes a very long way. Pairs especially well with the Mexican Cottage Pie as a counter to the richness of the mash.
