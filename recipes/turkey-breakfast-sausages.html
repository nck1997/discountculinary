Turkey Breakfast Sausages

Homemade breakfast sausage patties using lean turkey and a breakfast-spice profile.

Servings6
Calories / serving163
Protein / serving20g
Total980 kcal · 120g

Ingredients

  • Ground turkey
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Sage
  • Thyme
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Paprika
  • Maple syrup optional
  • Red pepper flakes optional

Steps

  1. Mix turkey with seasonings gently.
  2. Rest mixture briefly if time allows.
  3. Form into small patties.
  4. Cook in skillet until browned and fully cooked through.
  5. Serve with eggs, tacos, or bagels.

Notes

Easy meal prep protein that feels better than most store-bought turkey breakfast sausage.
