Lasagna Soup

Soup version of lasagna built off the turkey ragu base, finished with broken noodles and cheesy topping.

Servings6
Calories / serving392
Protein / serving38g
Total2350 kcal · 225g

Ingredients

  • Turkey ragu base
  • Chicken broth or water
  • Lasagna noodles, broken up
  • Cottage cheese or ricotta
  • Mozzarella
  • Parmesan
  • Italian herbs
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Bring turkey ragu up with broth to soup consistency.
  2. Add broken lasagna noodles and simmer until tender.
  3. Mix cottage cheese or ricotta with parmesan and pepper.
  4. Ladle soup into bowls and top with cheese mixture and mozzarella.
  5. Optional: broil individual bowls briefly or stir cheese in.

Notes

Very easy way to turn leftover ragu into a different meal without feeling repetitive.
